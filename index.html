Diesel refund

Diesel refund advisory for South African businesses

If you need diesel refund advice, the practical question is usually not whether a refund exists in theory, but whether your records, litres and site operations can prove eligible use under SARS scrutiny. Meridian provides evidence-led diesel refund advisory for mining, industrial and fuel-intensive operators, from first-time claim support through audit response.

What is the diesel refund?

The diesel refund is a SARS-administered scheme under the Customs and Excise Act. Qualifying VAT-registered businesses in sectors such as mining, farming and forestry can claim back part of the general fuel levy and RAF levy on diesel used for eligible on-land activities. Registration, logbooks and source documents are mandatory, and SARS can verify claims before approving refunds.

Who needs diesel refund advice?

Businesses most often seek diesel refund advice when preparing a first claim, responding to a SARS audit or verification, migrating to the new 2026 platform, or fixing gaps in logbooks, reconciliations and site controls. Meridian advises on the full picture: legal position, records and operational reality.
